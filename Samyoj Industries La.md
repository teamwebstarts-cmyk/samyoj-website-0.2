## 10. Industry FAQ Copy

### Which industries can use Samyoj?

Samyoj is useful for any team that manages customer conversations on WhatsApp. Strong fits include e-commerce, retail, education, real estate, healthcare and wellness, travel, SaaS, professional services, financial-service enquiries, automotive, recruitment, and local services.

### Is Samyoj only for marketing teams?

No. Marketing teams can run approved-template campaigns, but sales and support teams can also use the shared inbox, lead pipeline, tickets, AI bot, automations, and reporting to manage daily customer work.

### Can I customize workflows for my industry?

Yes. Build flows with triggers, questions, buttons, lists, forms, tags, conditions, delays, assignments, AI steps, webhooks, language paths, and human handoffs. This lets each business create journeys around its own intake and service process.

### Can different departments use Samyoj?

Yes. Teams can use departments, roles, permissions, availability, assignments, internal notes, and audit history to coordinate access and responsibility.

### Can Samyoj manage regulated industry workflows?

Samyoj can support customer communication, lead intake, scheduling requests, and operational follow-up in regulated industries.
