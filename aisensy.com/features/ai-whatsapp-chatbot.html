    FAQ

    Frequently asked questions answered

    What is the difference between an AI WhatsApp Chatbot and a regular WhatsApp chatbot?

    A regular chatbot follows predefined rules and fixed flows, answering only specific keywords or menu-based options. An AI WhatsApp Chatbot uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning to understand intent, context, and sentiment—allowing it to respond intelligently, personalize conversations, and improve over time.

    Is AiSensy’s AI WhatsApp Chatbot built on the official WhatsApp Business API?

    Yes. AiSensy’s AI WhatsApp Chatbot is powered by the official WhatsApp Business API, ensuring high message deliverability, enterprise-grade security, and full compliance with Meta’s policies.

    Can I integrate the AI WhatsApp Chatbot with my existing CRM, eCommerce platform, or other tools?

    Absolutely. AiSensy's AI WhatsApp Chatbot seamlessly integrates with popular CRM systems (like HubSpot, Salesforce), eCommerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento), payment gateways, and marketing automation tools. These integrations enable automated order updates, cart recovery, lead sync, customer data management, and personalized marketing campaigns—all through WhatsApp.

    Can I hand over conversations from the AI chatbot to a human agent?

    Yes. You can seamlessly transfer conversations from the AI chatbot to a live human agent whenever needed—ensuring smooth escalation for complex queries, sales conversations, or sensitive issues.

    Can WhatsApp AI Agents replace human agents completely?

    No. WhatsApp AI Agents excel at handling routine, repetitive queries and high-volume tasks. However, human agents are essential for complex, emotional, or escalated issues. Best practice is a hybrid model with smooth bot-to-human handoff.

    How much does it cost to set up an AI WhatsApp Chatbot?

    WhatsApp charges businesses based on WhatsApp Business Platform pricing (billing is determined by Meta and varies by market and message category). On top of that, your overall cost may include your WhatsApp Business API provider/platform plan (AiSensy) and any AI usage (if applicable). The best way to estimate total cost is to map your use case: support volume, outbound campaigns, and automation/AI coverage.

    How do I train the AI WhatsApp Chatbot for my business?

    Training your AI WhatsApp Chatbot with AiSensy is simple and requires no coding. You can upload your FAQs, knowledge base documents, product catalogs, and website content directly into the platform. The AI learns from your business data using natural language processing (NLP) and continuously improves through real customer interactions. You can also customize responses, set up specific conversation flows, and refine the bot's understanding over time.

    Can the AI WhatsApp Chatbot send rich messages like buttons, lists, images, and documents?

    Yes. You can use WhatsApp’s interactive and media message types (for example: buttons, list messages, images, videos, PDFs) to guide users through support and purchase journeys. A common best practice is: AI answers the question, then offers structured options (buttons/lists) to reduce back-and-forth and improve resolution rates.
