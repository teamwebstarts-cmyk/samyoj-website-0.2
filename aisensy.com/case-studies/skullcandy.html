    The Challenge

    Transforming Skullcandy’s Challenges into Triumphs

    Increasing Abandoned Carts

    Users visiting the website added products to their cart but did not complete the purchase. 

    Reduced ROI

    Sending personalized emails & SMS did not have an efficient enough ROI for Skullcandy.

    Siloed Customer Support

    Skullcandy had no direct source to communicate with customers in real-time. 1:1 Live Chat was not much effective via Website Chat! 

    The Solution

    How AiSensy Helped Skullcandy Overcome These Challenges

    25-40% Abandoned Carts Recovered

    To solve the dilemma of Abandoned Carts, AiSensy set up WhatsApp automation, connecting Skullcandy's Ecommerce store with AiSensy.

    This integration triggered an Abandoned Cart recovery notification 15-20 minutes after a user added a product to their cart & didn't complete the transaction. 

    This strategy helped Skullcandy recover 25-40% of Abandoned Carts. 

    Broadcast Promotional Offers to Unlimited Users in One Go

    Using AiSensy's intuitive platform, Skullcandy seamlessly broadcasted marketing and promotional offers to their entire customer base — reaching users even if they hadn’t added Skullcandy to their contacts.

    By adhering to WhatsApp’s best practices, Skullcandy successfully delivered WhatsApp messages to an unlimited number of users, all without the risk of being blocked.

    Automate Order & Delivery Updates on WhatsApp

    With AiSensy, Skullcandy has automated the sending of:

    • Order confirmations directly via WhatsApp, eliminating the need for emails or SMS.
    • Delivery updates in real time, ensuring that customers have quick and convenient access to information.
