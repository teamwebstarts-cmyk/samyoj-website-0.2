    Menu discovery and ordering on WhatsApp

    Let customers browse categories, bestsellers, and combos on WhatsApp and place orders quickly with guided flows—no app downloads, no friction.

    Cart recovery for incomplete orders

    Recover lost revenue by automatically reminding customers who started ordering but didn’t complete checkout. Offer a quick “Resume Order” CTA to drive conversions.

    Order confirmation & real-time status updates

    Automatically send order confirmation, preparation updates, out-for-delivery alerts, and ETA messages—reducing “Where is my order?” queries significantly.

    Address capture & delivery instructions collection

    Collect address, landmark, preferred delivery notes, and contact confirmation within the WhatsApp flow—reducing failed deliveries and support escalations.

    24/7 support for order issues

    Use a WhatsApp chatbot to handle common issues like order status, missing items, delivery delays, refunds, and cancellations. Escalate complex cases to agents instantly.

    Re-order and personalized recommendations

    Drive repeat purchases with one-tap reorders, “Order again” flows, and personalized suggestions based on previous orders, time of day, and preferences.

    Offers, coupons, and loyalty nudges

    Run segmented campaigns for lunch deals, weekend combos, and win-back coupons. Encourage loyalty sign-ups and reward redemptions directly inside WhatsApp.
