    FAQs

    Frequently asked questions answered

    What features does AiSensy offer for WhatsApp automation and marketing?

    AiSensy unifies WhatsApp marketing, customer support, and CRM. You get tools like no-code chatbot builders, multi-agent live chat, scheduleable broadcasts, smart audience segmentation, CTA-enabled notifications, and click-through analytics - all powered by the official WhatsApp Business API.

    What is WhatsApp Broadcasting in AiSensy?

    WhatsApp Broadcasting feature in AiSensy lets you send official, template-based messages to thousands (or even lakhs) of contacts at once using the WhatsApp Business API. With AiSensy, these broadcasts are delivered instantly, bypassing WhatsApp’s 256-contact limit on the regular app.

    How is AiSensy’s broadcasting different from regular WhatsApp forwarding?

    Regular WhatsApp forwarding is manual, limited to 256 contacts, and can get your account flagged. AiSensy uses the official WhatsApp Business API, ensuring higher deliverability, compliance, and advanced features like personalization, segmentation, and analytics.

    Does AiSensy support Click-to-WhatsApp ads, and what benefits do they bring?

    Yes, AiSensy enables you to build and run Click-to-WhatsApp ads directly from the platform. This drives 3× to 5× more leads and reduces cost per lead by up to 60%, helping you acquire quality leads along with automated lead segregation and campaign analytics.

    Can I collect payments and manage transactions within WhatsApp using AiSensy?

    Definitely. AiSensy supports in-app payments on WhatsApp via UPI, net banking, and cards (through Razorpay, PayU, etc.). You can send payment messages on WhatsApp, track payment status, retarget non-completers, and verify transactions in the user profile.

    What is WhatsApp Webviews and how does it improve conversions?

    WhatsApp Webviews let users access web pages directly within the chat—no external browser needed. This reduces drop-offs, boosts engagement, and keeps conversions high while preserving end-to-end encryption.

    How do AiSensy's Basic and Pro plans differ?

    The Basic plan covers unlimited broadcasting, automation, multi-agent chat, and basic analytics. The Pro plan adds advanced features - scheduling broadcasts, click tracking on CTAs, birthday automations, failed-message retries, extensive segmentation (via tags/attributes), and API/project access.
