# Section: Pricing

Source: `aisensy.com/pricing.html` + `aisensy.com/pricing/` (index.html, usd.html)

## What this section is about
Plans and WhatsApp message-cost explainer.

## Key copy extracted
- Free forever plan: free WhatsApp Business API access, zero upfront cost (perfect for SMBs).
- No setup/procurement fee — just a subscription fee.
- WhatsApp charges per message: ₹1.09/marketing message, ₹0.145/utility & auth message. Service conversations (replies) are free.
- Broadcast limits scale: 2000/day → 10,000 → 100,000 → infinite (based on volume sent in 7 days, upgrades in 24h).
- Pricing Calculator tool available (whatsapp-business-api-pricing-calculator.html).
