Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Samyoj may use cookies and similar technologies to make our website and Service work, remember preferences, understand usage, and protect against abuse.

Types of cookies

Essential cookies support core functions such as security, session management and load balancing. Preference cookies remember choices you make. Analytics cookies help us understand site performance and improve the experience. We use non-essential cookies only where required permissions have been obtained.

Your controls

You can manage or delete cookies in your browser settings. Blocking certain cookies may affect how the website or Service works. Where a cookie banner is provided, you can use it to change non-essential cookie preferences.

More information

For information on how we handle personal information, read our Privacy Policy. For cookie questions, contact us through the Contact page.
