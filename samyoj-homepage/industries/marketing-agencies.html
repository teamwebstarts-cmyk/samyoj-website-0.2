    WhatsApp Marketing FAQs

    Q. Can I sell products on WhatsApp?

    Yes, businesses can sell products directly on WhatsApp using the WhatsApp Business App or WhatsApp Business API. With features like product catalogs, shopping carts, automated chatbots, and integrated payment options, customers can browse products, ask questions, and complete purchases without leaving the chat.

    Q. How can eCommerce businesses use WhatsApp for sales?

    eCommerce brands can use WhatsApp to create a complete sales funnel, including:

    • Sending Click-to-WhatsApp Ads to generate leads

    • Sharing product catalogs and product recommendations

    • Automating conversations with AI chatbots

    • Sending abandoned cart reminders and order confirmations

    • Running broadcast campaigns for promotions and product launches

    These capabilities help businesses engage customers in real time and guide them from discovery to purchase within the same conversation.

    Q. Do customers need to leave WhatsApp to complete a purchase?

    Not necessarily. With WhatsApp commerce features, customers can browse products, add items to a cart, and complete purchases directly within the chat interface.

    Many businesses also integrate WhatsApp with payment gateways or eCommerce platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce, allowing users to complete checkout seamlessly.

    Q. Is WhatsApp marketing effective for eCommerce businesses?

    Yes. WhatsApp is one of the most effective marketing channels for eCommerce because it enables direct, personalized communication with customers. Businesses can send targeted messages, product recommendations, and exclusive offers to customers who have opted in, increasing engagement and repeat purchases.

    Q. How much does WhatsApp Marketing cost?

    WhatsApp Marketing is free if done using WhatsApp Business App. However, if you're using a WhatsApp API-based platform like Samyoj, you'll need to pay for platform charges (starting at ₹1500/ month) and also pay for WhatsApp Conversations (₹1.09/ Marketing Conversation, ₹0.145/ Utility & Authentication Conversation.

    Q. Is Samyoj an Official WhatsApp Marketing Software?

    Yes, Samyoj is an Official WhatsApp Business Solution Partner (BSP) built on WhatsApp Business APIs helping over 100,000+ businesses across 57+ countries grow with WhatsApp Marketing. 

    Q. Is there a free trial available for Samyoj?

    Yes! You can use Samyoj free for lifetime with our Forever free plan. To experience all powerful Samyoj features like broadcasting, chatbot, integrations, you can start your 14-day free trial - no credit card needed.

    Q. Is WhatsApp Marketing legal?

    Yes, WhatsApp Marketing is 100% legal when done via the Official WhatsApp Business API. Avoid using unofficial tools to prevent account bans or privacy issues.
