    Key Features of WhatsApp Business API for Retail

    Product Catalog & Order Placement

    Retailers can use WhatsApp to showcase their product catalogs, allowing customers to browse items, ask questions, and place orders directly through the app.

    By integrating shopping features, customers can easily add items to their cart and complete purchases without leaving WhatsApp.
    Learn More

    Personalized Promotions & Offers

    Send targeted promotions, discount codes, and exclusive deals directly to customers via WhatsApp.

    By using customer data, retailers can offer personalized recommendations based on previous purchases or browsing behavior, driving engagement and increasing sales.

    Click-to-WhatsApp Ads for 5x More Leads

    Retail businesses can use Click-to-WhatsApp ads to direct potential customers from social media channels like Facebook and Instagram.

    As users click on your Ads, they land on Whatsapp and you receive their name and WhatsApp number - this instantly generates leads and allows for personalized communication.
    Learn More

    Customer Support & Product Inquiries

    Provide instant customer support via WhatsApp Chatbots for product inquiries, store services, or return/exchange policies.

    For more complex questions, escalate to live agents, ensuring customers receive prompt assistance in real-time.

    Appointment Booking for In-Store Consultations

    Businesses like salons, spas, and boutiques can use WhatsApp to automate appointment bookings.

    Customers can schedule consultations, services, or private shopping sessions directly through the app, reducing the need for phone calls or email exchanges.

    Customer Feedback & Reviews

    After a purchase or service, retailers can use WhatsApp to request customer feedback or encourage reviews.

    Collecting feedback via WhatsApp is quick and convenient, improving customer relations and helping businesses gain valuable insights for improvement.
